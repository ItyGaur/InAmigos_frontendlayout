Who We Are

A Foundation Built on Friendship & Purpose

Founded on September 23, 2020, by Mr. Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation — whose name echoes "friends" — is a Section 8 non-profit licensed by the Central Government of India. Based in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh, we operate a network of volunteers across the country to address society's most pressing challenges.

Our Initiatives

Six Projects, One Mission

From classrooms to forests, from kitchens to clinics — every project closes a gap that India cannot afford to ignore.

Education
Project Bachpanshala

Nurturing Young Minds

Bridging educational gaps for underprivileged children through digital literacy, life skills training, and school-education support — ensuring no child is left behind.

📚 Digital literacy for rural children
Food & Relief
Project Sewa

Serving Humanity with Compassion

Providing nutritious meals and clothing to the underprivileged. Over 50,000 meals have been distributed — because hunger is a crisis that cannot wait.

🍱 50,000+ meals & clothes distributed
Women Empowerment
Project Udaan

Soaring Towards a Brighter Future

Collaborating with rural self-help groups to promote financial independence, skill development, and menstrual hygiene awareness for 900+ women across India.

👩 900+ girls empowered through skills
Animal Welfare
Project Jeev

Empowering Lives, Spreading Compassion

Dedicated to animal rescue, protection, and daily feeding of stray animals. Our volunteer network feeds 50+ stray animals every single day.

🐾 50+ stray animals fed daily
Environment
Project Prakriti

Plant for a Better Tomorrow

Advocating sustainability through sapling drives, eco-friendly agriculture support, and conservation campaigns — with over 20,000 trees planted across India.

🌱 20,000+ saplings planted
Skill Development
Project Vikas

Unlocking Potential, Building Careers

Internship programmes across digital marketing, content writing, finance, and more. In four years, we have onboarded and trained 30,000+ interns nationwide.

💼 30,000+ interns trained in 4 years
